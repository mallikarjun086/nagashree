Common First-Salary Traps

❌

"I'll start saving next month"

The most expensive lie you tell yourself. Month 1 sets the pattern.

✅ Fix: Auto-transfer on Day 1. Make it invisible.
❌

Buying everything on EMI

That ₹30,000 phone becomes ₹35,000 with interest. And you're locked in.

✅ Fix: Save for 3 months, buy with cash. No interest, no stress.
❌

Ignoring health insurance

One hospital visit can wipe out 6 months of savings. Check your company cover.

✅ Fix: Ask HR about your group health policy. Know what's covered.
❌

"My CTC is my real salary"

Budgeting with ₹50,000 when you get ₹38,000 leads to month-end panic.

✅ Fix: Always budget with in-hand. CTC is just a number on paper.
